Project Overview

What HealthRisk AI is and why it was built

HealthRisk AI builds a convergence system that translates clinical patient data into actionable financial risk signals. Rather than treating healthcare and finance as separate disciplines, the platform uses AI to create a bridge: patient readmission probabilities drive insurance premiums; hospital quality scores influence bond credit ratings; pharmaceutical trial signals guide portfolio allocation.

🏦 Health Insurance

Actuarial premium pricing powered by clinical trajectory features. Readmission risk directly adjusts individual premium calculations and IBNR loss reserves.

🏥 Hospital Credit Risk

Hospital bond credit scoring that uses clinical quality as leading indicators. CMS star ratings and readmission rates feed into PD default models.

💊 Pharma Portfolio

Risk-Adjusted Net Present Value (rNPV) valuation, clinical trial pipeline monitoring, and biomarker-aware portfolio optimization using Monte Carlo simulations.

🎮 HealthRisk Lab

Gamified investment simulation where players manage a $100M healthcare portfolio across 4 quarters, competing against an AI opponent across 11 shock scenarios.

🧠 Clinical AI Layers

Data Pipeline (src/data_pipeline/)
Ingests MIMIC-IV, FDA FAERS, WHO, ClinicalTrials.gov. Cleans lab outliers with physiological range filters. Computes Charlson Comorbidity Index from ICD-10 codes.
ClinicalBERT NLP (src/clinical_nlp/)
Fine-tuned BERT for clinical discharge notes. Extracts diagnoses, treatments & negations. Produces complexity embeddings (PCA-32) for the stacker.
Graph Attention Network (src/graph_nn/)
3-layer GAT with multi-head attention on a patient–disease–drug heterogeneous graph. Produces relational risk embeddings beyond tabular features.
Survival Models (src/survival/)
Cox PH and DeepSurv predict time-to-readmission. Financial variant predicts time-to-covenant-breach for hospital bond early warning.

💰 Financial Application Layers

Stacking Ensemble (src/ensemble/stacker.py)
Meta-learner (Logistic Regression) combines XGBoost, LightGBM, BERT embeddings, GNN embeddings, and DeepSurv hazards. Time-aware cross-validation prevents data leakage.
Insurance Actuarial (src/financial/insurance/)
GLM-based premium pricing using readmission probability as a risk multiplier. Chain Ladder and Bornhuetter-Ferguson for IBNR reserve estimation.
Hospital Credit (src/financial/credit_risk/)
Enhanced PD scorecard adding CMS stars and readmission rate as credit factors on top of operating margin & DSCR. Predicts rating downgrades 6–12 months ahead.
Pharma rNPV (src/financial/pharma/)
Monte Carlo rNPV valuation varying peak sales, patent runway, and launch delay. Clinical Alpha portfolio optimizer uses FDA safety events and trial enrollment to rebalance biopharma weights.

Feature Engineering

How raw clinical data is transformed into predictive signals

👤 Patient Demographics & Baseline Risk

Age splines and buckets for non-linear age effects. One-hot encoded insurance payer mix (Medicare, Medicaid, Private, Uninsured).

Age Splines Charlson CCI (0–15) Payer Mix (OHE) Gender · Race

🧪 72-Hour Lab Trends

Time-series features extracted from a 72-hour sliding window on key lab tests: Creatinine, Hemoglobin, Sodium, WBC, Lactate, BNP.

Last Value Mean & Std Dev Rate of Change (Slope) Min / Max Extremes

💊 Polypharmacy Risk Metrics

Medication count and polypharmacy flags derived from NDC/ATC codes. Drug-drug interaction risk score for adverse event probability.

Med Count Polypharmacy Flag (≥5) Hyperpolypharmacy (≥10) DDI Score

🏨 Healthcare Utilization History

Lookback metrics capturing how frequently and intensively the patient uses healthcare services — strong predictors of readmission.

Prior Admissions Cumulative LOS (12m) ICU Admits Count

🏦 Hospital Bond Risk Features

Clinical quality metrics embedded into financial bond analysis. Enhanced DSCR accounts for value-based care penalty exposure.

Enhanced DSCR CMI-Adjusted Cash CMS Star Rating 30d Readmission Rate

🧬 Charlson Comorbidity Index (CCI)

Standard clinical tool mapping ICD-10 diagnoses to weighted mortality risk. Sum of all weights gives a single patient frailty score.

Weight 1 → Heart Failure, DM Weight 2 → Renal Disease, Tumors Weight 3 → Liver Disease Weight 6 → AIDS/Metastatic

Financial Applications

How clinical AI signals translate into financial risk models

🏦 Insurance Actuarial Premium Pricing

Clinical readmission probability is used as a direct multiplier on the baseline premium, enabling risk-stratified underwriting at the patient level.

Premiumadj = Baseline × (1 + α · Preadmit)

IBNR reserves computed using Chain Ladder (CDFs from age-to-age link ratios) and Bornhuetter-Ferguson (blends with a priori loss ratios for volatile years).

🏥 Hospital Credit Risk & Municipal Bonds

Standard credit models use only financial metrics. HealthRisk AI adds clinical quality signals as leading indicators — predicting downgrades 6–12 months ahead of traditional audits.

CreditScore = f(Margin, DSCR) − β·ReadmitRate + γ·CMSStars

High readmission rates trigger CMS value-based purchasing penalties → reduced revenue → covenant breach risk detected early by DeepSurv survival model.

💊 Pharmaceutical Pipeline rNPV Valuation

Drug R&D assets are valued using Risk-Adjusted NPV — discounting future cash flows by the probability of successfully completing each clinical trial phase.

rNPV = Σ [ (CashFlowt × Psuccess) / (1 + r)t ] − R&D Cost
